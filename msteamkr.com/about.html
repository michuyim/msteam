Profile

  • Name: Wi Ha Joon
  • Native name:
  • A.K.A: Wi Ha Joon, wi Ha Joon, Wi Hajun
  • Nationality: South Korean
  • Gender: Male
  • Born: August 5, 1991



Wi Hyeon-i(birthname) was born in Soan, Wando County. His parents were abalone farmers, during an interview Ha Jun mentions that his family went into debt as the business was going through some difficulties, this forced him to learn to be independent and fend for himself, he has seen paid the loans off and gotten his family's farm out of debt.


He has a degree in Theather arts and film from the Sungkyul university, he always wanted to be a dancer and never quite had plans to act. Today he is not just an actor but one of the leading actors in kdrama. He has recently discovered his natural talent to model and is the brand ambassador to Louis Vuitton, Tag Heuer, Tiffany & Co., GQ and more.

Personal Statement Wi Ha Jun

I want to express my deepest gratitude for the love and support you have shown me throughout my journey as an actor. Your encouragement and unwavering belief have been the driving force behind my passion for acting.


From a young age, I knew that storytelling was my true calling. Exploring the emotions of different characters and bringing their stories to life on screen brings me immense joy. Acting, to me, is not just a profession but an art form to convey the complexities of the human experience.


Connecting with each and every one of you has been a rewarding experience. My growth and accomplishments are all thanks to your empowering support. With each project, I challenge myself and strive to surpass my limits, always eager to learn something new. I believe in the power of storytelling to inspire, heal, and stimulate people's thoughts. My wish is for my work to touch the hearts of audiences worldwide and leave a lasting impact.


None of this would be possible without you, my dear fans and supporters. Your passion and encouragement fuel my acting endeavors. Your letters, messages, and kind words mean the world to me and serve as a great source of strength. I promise to give my best in every performance.


I am excited and honored to share this incredible journey with you all. Your presence is meaningful and treasured. I am forever grateful for the opportunity to connect with you through acting, and let us continue to dream, believe, and inspire one another.



With all my heart,

Wi Ha Jun

Details

Wi Ha Jun is a South Korean actor and model. His real name is actually Wi Hyeon-i but he goes by the stage name Wi Ha Jun/Joon. Born on August 5, 1991, Wi Ha Joon was raised on his family's abalone farm in Soan-myeon, Wando County, South Korea. This subsequently left him with his favorite acquired taste of instant noodles with abalone.


A member of the dance team in middle and high school, Wi Ha Joon first dreamt of being a dancer on stage in Seoul as he was very passionate about dancing. He attended various auditions held by Korea's big entertainment giants such as SM Entertainment and JYP. He even managed to pass the first round of SM Entertainment's auditions but failed the second round. This was when he decided to pursue acting instead.


Wi Ha Joon proves to be an extremely tenacious and persevering young man in his career. To pursue his acting dreams, Wi Ha Joon got rid of his regional dialect and learned to speak in Seoul dialect. To stop himself from lapsing back to his regional dialect, he took several extreme measures such as not speaking to his hometown friends for close to a year. Fortunately, his hard work paid off and he made his acting debut in 2012.

To be on the big screens, Wi Ha Joon pretended that he could drive and smoke during an audition as the role required him to. To his great surprise, he passed the audition. Wi Ha Joon had to get his driving license and learn how to smoke three weeks before filming his debut film in 2018. He subsequently gave up smoking after the filming ended.


Wi Ha Joon also tries his best to portray his roles in dramas as accurately as possible. In one of his films, he lost 13kg to portray a wiry murderer character. He also studied the profiles of many serial murderers to find common traits amongst them to give his best performance.
